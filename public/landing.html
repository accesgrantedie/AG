FAQ

Common questions

Who can sign up as a student? +

Any student enrolled in a Computer Science or related degree at an Irish university. We verify enrolment before your profile goes live.

How much does it cost for businesses? +

A flat monthly subscription — no placement fee, no cut of the project cost. Students keep 100% of what they earn.

What is GradStart and how do I access it? +

GradStart subsidises up to 50% of a graduate's first-year salary when hired full-time by an Irish SME. We handle the application end-to-end.

How does the AI assessment work? +

When you post a role, our AI builds a tailored technical test from your requirements. You see each student's score, time, and answer breakdown before deciding to interview.

What if the project doesn't work out? +

Our milestone system catches issues early. If a project fails, our team steps in and businesses aren't charged for incomplete work.
